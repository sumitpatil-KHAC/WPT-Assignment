From Tractors to Supercars

Born from ambition and defiance, Lamborghini was created to challenge the limits of performance and redefine automotive excellence.

Ferruccio Lamborghini

The Visionary Founder

Ferruccio Lamborghini, originally a successful tractor manufacturer, founded Automobili Lamborghini in 1963 with a bold vision to create the perfect grand touring car.

Driven by passion and determination, he transformed a challenge into one of the most iconic automotive brands in the world.

The Raging Bull

Inspired by Ferruccio’s zodiac sign Taurus, the bull symbolizes power, aggression, and dominance.

Mission

Driving Humans Beyond — pushing limits through bold innovation and unexpected experiences.

Naming Legacy

Models like Miura and Aventador are named after legendary fighting bulls.
